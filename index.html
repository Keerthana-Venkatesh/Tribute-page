Tribute to Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli, born on November 5, 1988, is an Indian cricketer and one of the most consistent batsmen in the history of cricket. Known for his aggressive batting and leadership skills, he has broken numerous records in all formats of the game. Kohli is widely regarded as one of the best chasers in limited-overs cricket, earning him the nickname 'Chase Master.'

Over his illustrious career, he has won numerous awards, including the ICC Cricketer of the Year multiple times, and has captained India to the top of the ICC Test rankings.
